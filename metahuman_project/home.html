Plan

The Metahuman Project is going to consist of a number of stories in a shared Universe. It will be my take on worlds like the Marvel Cinematic Universe or the Arrowverse, populated by original characters and stories.

As of right now, I have plans for the first two stories and am in the process of writing the first, which will go here when it's ready.
