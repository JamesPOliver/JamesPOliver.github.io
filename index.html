Welcome

My name is James Oliver, and I'm a Computing Student who writes as a hobby.
This site is going up because I thought maybe some people may enjoy reading my
work rather than it just being written and then deleted when I need space on whatever
device I wrote it on.

Ever since Secondary School I've been a big fan of both the Arrowverse and the Marvel
Cinematic Universe. Both are interconnected superhero franchises that has superheroes telling
Their own stories, but when they need to they always come together to fight things that they never
could alone.

I've also noticed that there aren't many superheroes based in Ireland, where I live. This led me to write
my own take on the genre, which I did several years ago. Back then my audience was one of my closest friends
and he always told me that I should put my work out there. At one point, I had like twenty different heroes with
their own stories and things. I came across these recently, and decided to rewrite them.

I hope you enjoy reading my work as much as I enjoy writing it.

Release

I plan to release my work in an episodic format, where each story consists of a set of episodes released over some
interval that I will decide upon. I'm hoping to have the first story, Blizzard, ready to post by August.

Yes. I did say "each story". I plan to release multiple stories which I hope people like.
